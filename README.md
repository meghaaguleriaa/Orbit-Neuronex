# 🚀 ORBIT — The Autonomous Hiring Operating System

![Orbit Hero](orbit%20screenshots/dashboard.jpeg)

### Transforming Recruitment from Reactive Filtering to Predictive Autonomous Hiring

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Traditional recruitment is slow, reactive, and heavily dependent on manual processes. Recruiters spend significant time screening resumes, evaluating candidates, coordinating interviews, and making hiring decisions with limited predictive insights.

As organizations scale, these inefficiencies lead to:

* Delayed hiring cycles
* Overlooked talent
* Increased recruitment costs
* Poor hiring decisions
* Inconsistent candidate evaluation

Orbit addresses these challenges by introducing an autonomous, AI-powered recruitment ecosystem capable of discovering, evaluating, simulating, debating, and recommending candidates with minimal human intervention.

---

# 🌟 What is Orbit?

Orbit is an Autonomous Hiring Operating System designed to transform recruitment from a manual workflow into an intelligent, agent-driven decision-making process.

Instead of simply matching resumes to job descriptions, Orbit predicts:

* Who should be hired
* Why they should be hired
* How they will perform in the future
* How they will impact existing teams
* Whether they should be trained rather than rejected

Orbit shifts recruitment from reactive filtering to predictive hiring.

# 🎯 Key Innovations

## 🧬 Digital Twin Technology

Orbit creates intelligent representations of:

* Candidates
* Roles
* Organizations

These Digital Twins allow the platform to perform deeper compatibility analysis beyond keyword matching.

---

## 🤖 Multi-Agent Intelligence

Orbit utilizes a network of specialized AI agents:

* Talent Discovery Agent
* Candidate Intelligence Agent
* Predictive Match Agent
* Autonomous Interview Agent
* Hiring Orchestrator Agent

Each agent contributes unique insights throughout the hiring lifecycle.

---

## 🏛 Orbit Decision Chamber

One of Orbit's most innovative features.

Instead of relying on a single AI score, multiple specialized AI agents debate candidate suitability before a final hiring recommendation is generated.

Participants include:

* Technical Agent
* Culture Agent
* Growth Agent
* Risk Agent

The Hiring Orchestrator moderates the discussion and produces the final verdict.
